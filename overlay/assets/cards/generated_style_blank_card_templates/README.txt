Blank card templates based on the generated sci-fi design.

Included:
- common_blank_template.png
- uncommon_blank_template.png
- rare_blank_template.png
- epic_blank_template.png
- legendary_blank_template.png

These are raster PNG templates:
- Only the large top image window is transparent.
- Main card body remains filled/dark.
- Top small decorative lines have been removed as much as possible.
- Bottom styling remains.
- Placeholder manufacturer/logo text has been removed/covered.

Use in Affinity:
1. Place your ship image underneath the template.
2. Add your own text layers.
3. Export final card PNG.
